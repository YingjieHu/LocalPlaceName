 Welcome Home!
 This is a 825 Sq. Ft., 2 Bedroom, 1 Bath, Apartment Home.
 
 Parkside at Mirabeau in the beautiful Spokane Valley offers one, two and three bedroom apartment homes for rent. Each floor plan provides a full size washer and dryer, kitchen with all appliances, air-conditioning, and a large patio or balcony. Our community provides a fitness facility for resident use, a pool, and playground in addition to a resident business center. Ask about garage availability. Located just minutes off of I-90 in Spokane Valley, Washington. Close to the Valley Mall, Industrial Park, beautiful Mirabeau park and Centennial Trail. Our location is perfect for the active resident who loves to run or walk and enjoy parks and outdoor life. Also, close to Center Place and the outdoor water park. Our 3 bedrooms are reserved for households of 4 or more residents.
